Hero Data Protection Policy

At Monster Heroes of Myths, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you engage with our website and game.

Information We Collect

We may collect personal details such as your username, email address, and device information when you register or play Monster Heroes of Myths. We also gather non-personal data including browser type, IP address, and in-game performance statistics.

How We Use Your Information

Your information helps us improve game features, provide customer support, notify you about updates and events, and analyze gameplay trends. We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

Data Security

We apply robust security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. However, please note that no online transmission or storage system is completely secure.

Third-Party Services

Our game and website may integrate with third-party services that collect certain information. These services have their own privacy policies which govern how they manage your data.

Children's Privacy

Monster Heroes of Myths is not intended for children under 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under this age. If you believe your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be posted here, and your continued use of our game and website constitutes acceptance of those changes.

Contact Us

For questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, please reach out to us through the contact information on our Contact page.
