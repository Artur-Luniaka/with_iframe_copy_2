Hero Cookie Collection Policy

This Cookie Policy explains how Monster Heroes of Myths uses cookies and similar technologies to recognize you when you visit our website. It explains what these technologies are and why we use them, as well as your rights to control our use of them.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small data files stored on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. Cookies are widely used by website owners to make their websites operate efficiently, and to provide analytics and reporting information.

Types of Cookies We Use

We use the following types of cookies:

  • Essential Cookies: These cookies are necessary for the website to function correctly and cannot be disabled in our systems.
  • Performance Cookies: These cookies help us count visits and traffic sources to measure and enhance the performance of our site.
  • Functionality Cookies: These cookies enable the website to provide improved functionality and personalization.
  • Targeting Cookies: These cookies may be set by our advertising partners through our site to build a profile of your interests.

How to Control Cookies

You can configure your web browser settings to accept or reject cookies. If you choose to block cookies, you may still use our website, but some features or areas may be limited.

Changes to This Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy occasionally to reflect changes in the cookies we use or for legal or operational reasons. Please review this Cookie Policy regularly to stay informed about how we use cookies and related technologies.
